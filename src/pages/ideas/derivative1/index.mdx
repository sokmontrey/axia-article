Consider the [function](/ideas/function1) `f(x) = 2x`. Let's plug in some values of `x` and see what we get.

| `x`  | `f(x)` | 
|:--| :-: |
| 1    | 2      | 
| 2    | 4      | 
| 3    | 6      | 
| 4    | 8      | 
| 5    | 10     |
